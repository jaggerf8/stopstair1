So What is This?

Stop and Stair is a way to personalize your very own staircase! You can choose one of the images in our selection, or upload your own! We'll personalize the printout to match the dimensions of your stairs and ensure look looks and feels just the way you want it!

Personalized

We provide ways to customize the print to match the exact dimension of your stairs! Simply pop a picture and we'll do the rest!

Creative

You can choose any image you want, as long as it yours and not copyrighted. This means you can have your pet dog, cat, or loved ones decorating your stairs at home!

Relaxing

Have these stairs look just the way you like it!

Convenient

You can choose one of our pre-made stairs as well. All you have to do is take a picture of your staircase and we can calculate the dimensions!

Colorful

We also have a wide range of filters you can apply to your images to make sure you get the exact look and color you want!

Magical

When it comes down to it, having your own personalized staircase is every bit as magical as you can imagine.
